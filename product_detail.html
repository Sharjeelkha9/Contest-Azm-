Bamboo Palm

With dense foliage and lush fronds, the Bamboo Palm makes a statement. An air-purifying plant adaptable to low light, this palm can reach heights of up to 8 feet tall in the right conditions.

1500

In Stock

This is a beautiful plant that can thrive indoors or outdoors. It requires moderate sunlight and watering once a week. Ideal for homes and offices to bring a touch of nature indoors. The Bamboo Palm is a tropical indoor houseplant that compliments any space. Not to be confused with real bamboo, this plant is low maintenance and easy to care for. Native to the forests of Mexico and Central America, Bamboo Palms grow in the shade of larger trees unlike other palms, which makes them adaptable to less-than-ideal lighting conditions. It is a great choice for the home or office because it rates highly on NASA’s list of air-purifying plants. Please note: The variation of leaf splits and shape for the Bamboo Palm may vary

  • Height: 30-40 cm
  • Watering: Once a week
  • Sunlight: Moderate
  • Soil: Well-drained potting mix
  • Growth Rate: Moderate
